PROBABILITY:

We use probability modal verbs may, might and could – to say that something is possible, but not certain. Look at some examples:

They may come by car. (= Maybe they will come by car.)
They might be at home. (= Maybe they are at home.)
If we don't hurry, we could be late. (= Maybe we will be late.)

*Expressions that are often used to talk about probabilities:

  • Probably
  • Likely / unlikely
  • It looks like

Potential threats to a computer function

Reflected glare occurs when light from a source, such as a window or a bright lamp, reflects off a computer screen. It can cause discomfort, eye strain, and difficulty in reading or viewing content on the screen. Adjust the position of the screen or use anti-glare filters to minimize reflected glare.

Scandisk (or CHKDSK on Windows) is a utility that checks and repairs file system errors on a computer's hard drive. It is typically used to address disk-related problems, such as bad sectors or file system corruption. Running Scandisk helps maintain the integrity of the file system and ensures the proper functioning of the hard drive.

Overvoltage occurs when there is an excessive voltage or electrical potential difference in a circuit or electronic device. It can damage electronic components, including computer hardware, leading to malfunctions or permanent damage. Use surge protectors or voltage regulators to prevent overvoltage and protect electronic devices.

Unauthorized software refers to programs or applications that are installed on a computer without proper permission. Using unauthorized software may violate copyright laws, compromise system security, and lead to software-related problems. Ensure all software used on a computer is properly licensed.

Prohibition modal verbs

In the unit before you learned some rules that the director Peter of Tech Innovation said you must not or cannot do during your work journey! In other words, you read what is prohibited to do in the company. Now you will learn about useful modal verbs to describe prohibition.

Pin 1: Prohibition modal verbs are used to express a prohibition or a strong advice against doing something. The most common prohibition modal verbs in English are "must not" and "cannot."

Pin 2: 'Can't' and 'mustn't' (must not) are used to show that something is prohibited (not allowed). 'Can't' usually gives the idea of something that is against the rules. Mustn’t usually means that it is the speaker who is setting the rule.

Pin 3: Examples:
You can't go into that restaurant without a tie.
You can't drive in this country unless you are over eighteen.
You mustn't use your phone in class.

ODBC

ODBC, ou Open Database Connectivity, é um conjunto de classes-modelo para conexão disponível em qualquer linguagem de programação, diferindo-se da JDBC, que é exclusiva para Java.

JDBC

JDBC, ou Java EE Database Connectivity, é uma API (application programming interfaces) que possibilita a interação do código Java com um banco de dados, na qual as instruções SQL são executadas por meio de um driver, composto por um grupo de classes e interfaces desenvolvido em Java. Esse processo possibilita a execução de instruções SQLs que se conectarão com a base de dados, permitindo a manipulação dos valores.

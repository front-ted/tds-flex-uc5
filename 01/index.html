Introductions

Watch the video of Giselle arriving at the summit in Houston, TX. Who are they talking to?

Receptionist: Good morning and welcome to Houston Summit! How can I help you?

Giselle: Good morning! My name is Giselle and I would like to register for the summit and get my badge.

Receptionist: Excellent! My name is Andrew and I will help you with that. It’s very nice to meet you, Giselle.

Giselle: Nice to meet you, too.

Receptionist: Now, I will need some information to complete your registration. So, your first name is Giselle. What’s your last name?

Giselle: My last name is Souza.

Receptionist: Souza...how do you spell that?

Giselle: S-O-U-Z-A.

Receptionist: Alright. How old are you, Giselle?

Giselle: I’m twenty-five years old.

Receptionist: Okay, twenty-five...and what’s your nationality?

Giselle: I’m Brazilian. I’m from Erechim.

Receptionist: That’s great. I went to South Summit last year and I loved it! What’s your address in Brazil?

Giselle: It’s 350 Rubem Berta Street.

Receptionist: And what’s your zip code?

Giselle: It’s 75320-529.

Receptionist: Thank you. And what’s your cell phone number?

Giselle: It’s 55 (that’s the code for Brazil) 54-99870-4423.

Receptionist: Okay. 55-54-99870-4423. And what’s your passport number?

Giselle: My passport number is JSJ-7650.

Receptionist: Sorry, could you repeat that, please?

Giselle: Yes. It’s JSJ-7650.

Receptionist: Thanks, Giselle. For now, that is all I need. If you need something else, just let me know and I will assist you with that. Enjoy your time here at the event! Bye!

Giselle: Thank you! Bye.

ODBC

ODBC, ou Open Database Connectivity, é um conjunto de classes-modelo para conexão disponível em qualquer linguagem de programação, diferindo-se da JDBC, que é exclusiva para Java.

JDBC

JDBC, ou Java EE Database Connectivity, é uma API (application programming interfaces) que possibilita a interação do código Java com um banco de dados, na qual as instruções SQL são executadas por meio de um driver, composto por um grupo de classes e interfaces desenvolvido em Java. Esse processo possibilita a execução de instruções SQLs que se conectarão com a base de dados, permitindo a manipulação dos valores.
